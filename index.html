About Us

So you came here. It's strange, but in the same time we are truly glad that you want to join us. So, without long introductions, let's get started! Who we are? Why are we exist? How long infinity is? We can't give a certain answer, but we love to talk and think about it. Here is the place for mental rest, and your opportunity tho share your opinion with others.

Cozy and friendly atmosphere, cookies, warmth and a lot of debates! Nobody will be ofended, just discuss and tell your unpopular opinion. We will listen carefully, and who knows, maybe you will grant us with enlightement. Also, once per month we have very interesting lections, where you will find a lot of new thouths about this world. Join us right now, 'cause we wait for you!
